Visualising Victims of
🚨 Selected Major Offences in Singapore (2011-2019) 🚨

Data from data.gov.sg

While the number of victims of crimes of 🔪 violent nature (Murder, Serious Hurt, Rioting) and 👺 thieving nature (Robbery, Snatch Thief) have been constant or decreasing, the number of victims of crimes of 🍆 sexual nature (Cheating, Rape, Outrage of Modesty) has been increasing over the period of 9 years.

👨🏻 Men are generally more susceptible to being victims of crime, with the exception of: Rape, Outrage of Modesty, and Snatch Theft, where 👩🏻 Women are more susceptible.
